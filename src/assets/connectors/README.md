# Connector logo assets

These small PNG marks are local UI-identification assets for branded connectors that are not
available in the installed `react-icons` package. They were retrieved from the corresponding
provider domains through Google's favicon service on 2026-07-15:

- `brightcove.com`, `buzzsprout.com`, `canva.com`, `captivate.fm`, `descript.com`, `frame.io`
- `medal.tv`, `mux.com`, `podbean.com`, `restream.io`, `riverside.fm`, `sproutvideo.com`
- `squadcast.fm`, `streamyard.com`, `transistor.fm`, `vidyard.com`, `zencastr.com`

All names and marks remain the property of their respective owners. They are not Vidrial brand
assets and must only be used to identify their matching connector.
